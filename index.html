About

I am a dedicated microbiologist with a strong academic foundation, holding both a Master of Science (MSc) and a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Microbiology from Jagannath University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am pursuing a PhD in Microbiology at Texas A&M University and working in Dr. Koch's lab. My research focuses on the dynamic regulation of Type IV Pili in Pseudomonas aeruginosa, aiming to uncover critical insights into bacterial behavior and pathogenesis. As a self-motivated and dedicated learner, I am passionate about advancing our understanding of microbiology. My long-term goal is to make meaningful contributions to the scientific community and address global challenges through innovative microbiological research.
